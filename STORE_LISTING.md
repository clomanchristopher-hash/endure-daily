## 4. Full App Store description

> **Grow in Faith. Move with Purpose. Live with Discipline.**
>
> Endure Daily is a calm, encouraging daily companion for Christians who want to
> grow spiritually and physically — one faithful step at a time.
>
> Each day brings a complete rhythm in one place:
>
> • **Scripture of the Day** — a verse to center your morning (Berean Standard Bible)
> • **Short Devotion** — a focused, encouraging reading
> • **Prayer** — a daily prompt to still your heart
> • **Reflection** — answer a question and save it privately; it flows straight into your Journal
> • **Today's Progress** — check off Scripture, Devotion, Prayer, and your workout, and keep your streak alive
>
> **Move with Purpose.**
> The Move tab gives you one clear assignment for the day. Choose your lane:
>
> • **Foundation** — lighter movement like walking, mobility, stretching, and beginner-friendly strength
> • **Challenge** — higher-effort movement like running, gym, and full-body strength
>
> A simple built-in timer and a Complete button make it easy to show up — and
> finishing updates your daily progress.
>
> **Movement Journeys.**
> Explore structured **Running** and **Strength** journeys inside the Move tab.
> Free previews are available now — start a **First 5K Preview** or a **Foundation
> Strength Preview** and follow a guided week. Full guided premium journeys are
> being prepared for a future release.
>
> **Journal your walk.**
> Your reflections and notes are kept privately, organized by date, so you can
> look back and see how you've grown.
>
> **Built to feel calm and grounded.**
> A warm, earth-tone design, an encouraging tone, and a gentle daily celebration
> when you complete everything — no clutter, no noise.
>
> **Private by default.**
> Endure Daily works fully offline. Your data stays on your device unless you
> choose to create an account to sync across devices. No ads. No third-party
> tracking.
>
> Endure Daily is free to use.
>
> Train your body. Anchor your soul. One day at a time.

## 8. App Review notes (draft)

> Endure Daily is a faith-based daily growth app. It provides Scripture, a short
> devotion, prayer, a reflection prompt, journaling, daily movement assignments,
> and simple progress tracking (a daily checklist and streaks).
>
> No account is required to review the app — it works fully offline and can be
> used immediately on launch. Creating an account is optional and only enables
> syncing data across devices.
>
> **Premium Journeys are visible as future-release items only. They are not
> purchasable.** Tapping a locked premium journey shows a "Premium Journey
> Preview / available in a future release" message and cannot be started.
>
> **Payments are not active. There is no Stripe, Apple In-App Purchase,
> RevenueCat, pricing, or paywall anywhere in the app.** There is nothing to
> buy and no checkout of any kind.
>
> Scripture quotations are from the Berean Standard Bible (BSB), which is freely
> available for use.
>
> Support contact: clomanchristopher@gmail.com
